Consumer Groups

  • Center for Democracy and Technology (CDT)
  • National Partnership for Women and Families
  • Patient Privacy Rights (PPR)
  • Society for Participatory Medicine (S4PM)

Government

  • Department of Defense (DOD) working with Sysnet International: Sysnet International has found that at least 7 to 8 attributes are needed to address geographic variability. The project with the DOD did not develop a standard list of data attributes.
  • Social Security Administration (SSA): SSA recommends that ONC explore additional data attributes beyond the basics of the patient identification segment in HL7 to see if there could be other attributes that improve the patient patch.
